# Business Process Analysis – UMKM Toko Anugrah

## Project Overview
This project focuses on business process analysis of **Toko Anugrah**, a micro, small, and medium enterprise (UMKM) engaged in the sale of bakery ingredients and food packaging supplies. The study aims to document and analyze existing business processes to identify inefficiencies and propose improvement opportunities to support business sustainability.

This project was developed as part of an academic group assignment in the Information Systems program.

---

## Background
UMKM play a significant role in Indonesia’s economic growth by contributing to employment creation and poverty reduction. However, many UMKM still rely on manual business processes, which can limit operational efficiency and service quality.

Toko Anugrah currently manages inventory, ordering, and transaction processes manually. While the business continues to operate, there is a growing need to improve process efficiency and management effectiveness.

---

- Limited process efficiency and control
